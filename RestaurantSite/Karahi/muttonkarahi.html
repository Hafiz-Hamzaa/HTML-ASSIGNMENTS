Chicken Biryani Chicken Biryani is a delicious and popular dish loved by people all around the world.
It is a flavorful combination of rice, chicken, and aromatic spices. This dish is known for its rich taste
and mouthwatering aroma. To make chicken biryani, tender pieces of chicken are marinated with spices
like turmeric, cumin, and coriander. The chicken is then cooked with fragrant basmati rice, onions,
tomatoes, and a blend of spices like cinnamon, cloves, and cardamom. Saffron or food color is
sometimes added to give the rice a beautiful golden color. Chicken biryani is often served with
raita, which is a yogurt-based side dish, and sometimes with fresh salad or pickles.
It is a perfect meal for special occasions, family dinners, or celebrations.
This hearty and satisfying dish brings joy to every bite, making it a favorite for food lovers everywhere!
